Greek Horiatiki Salad

This is the one and only Greek salad recipe that you'll ever need! Enjoy this Greek salad for summer BBQs, Greek dinner parties, picnics, and more. Ripe, red tomatoes will yield the best result. Serve with loads of good crusty bread to mop up the gorgeous juices.

Ingredents

  • 5 large tomatoes
  • 1 small red onion, thinly sliced
  • sea salt to taste
  • 1 ½ teaspoons dried oregano
  • 1 (8 ounce) slice feta cheese
  • ½ cucumber, sliced (Optional)
  • 1 small green bell pepper, sliced (Optional)
  • ¼ cup Greek black olives
  • 3 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil

    • Directions

    1. Step 1

      Cut your tomatoes into wedges over the bowl that you're serving the salad in, so that any extra juices land in the bowl. Add onion slices. Liberally season with salt and oregano. Mix and allow tomatoes to sit at room temperature for 20 to 30 minutes, up to 2 hours, so that the salt releases the tomatoes' juices.

    3. Step 2

      Mix the cucumber, bell pepper, and olives just before serving. Top with feta cheese and drizzle with olive oil.
